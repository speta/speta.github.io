Membership

MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

Market Development

With the funding and support from government agencies e.g. the International Enterprise Singapore, SPETA organises regular trade fairs and missions for members to expand their market bases.

International Cooperation

SPETA has established a close working relationship with other trade associations world wide. This provides members the opportunities to interact and explore areas of business cooperation for mutual benefits.

Seminars, Talks and Workshops

SPETA invites local and foreign experts e.g. to conduct talks, seminars to assist members to keep abreast with the latest developments in technology and management.

Training Courses

SPETA monitors the training needs of the precision engineering and technology industry and organises skill upgrading courses with relevant institutions for members.

TYPE OF MEMBERSHIPS

Corporate Membership

Meant for companies engaged in manufacturing or providing services for the engineering and technology industry.
