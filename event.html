MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

Market Development

With the funding and support from the International Enterprise Singapore, SPETA organises regular trade missions for members to broaden their market bases. It recommends relevant and important overseas trade fairs for membersâ€™ participation.

International Co operation

SPETA has established a close working relationship with other trade association world wide. This provides members the opportunities to interact and explore areas of business co operation for mutual benefits.

Seminars, Talks and Workshops

SPETA invites local and foreign experts to conduct seminars regularly, to assist members to keep abreast with the latest developments in technology and management.

Training Courses

SPETA monitors the training needs of the P.E. industry and organises skill upgrading courses by relevant institutions for members.

TYPES OF MEMBERSHIPS

There are three types of memberships in the Association:

Corporate Membership

Meant for companies engaged in manufacturing or providing services for the engineering and tooling industry.

Advisory Membership

For non-profit voluntary organisations of national standing.

Honorary Membership

For persons who have contributed significantly towards the objectives of the Association.
